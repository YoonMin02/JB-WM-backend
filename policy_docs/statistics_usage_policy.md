# Statistics Usage Policy

Population statistics are anchors for comparison and scenario framing. They are not personal facts.

When using statistics:
- include source and as-of date when available
- separate customer-specific data from population averages
- do not infer a diagnosis from a population statistic
- do not present a statistic as a guarantee of cost, outcome, or eligibility

Use statistics to explain uncertainty and ranges. Combine them with customer context only for financial planning,
insurance review, liquidity checks, and resilience scenarios.
